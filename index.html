About Kevin

I’m Kevin David Weber, and I am both a Web Developer and a Creator. I have hard skills in HTML, CSS, Java Script along with 16 years of hands-on experience in video production and filmmaking. As a Web Developer I focus on applying these skills to create vivid web pages that are both responsive and accessible. I have a drive for lifelong learning and developing practical hands-on skills. I have an eye for detail and a hunger for knowledge. I have practical experience in accomplishing my goals in individual, collaborative and managerial roles. I bring drive, creativity and enthusiasm to any team and role.
